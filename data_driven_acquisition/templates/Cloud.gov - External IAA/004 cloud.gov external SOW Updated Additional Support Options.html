STATEMENT OF WORK FOR TTS SERVICES TO BE PROVIDED TO AGENCY, OFFICE

1.0. BACKGROUND

The U.S. General Services Administration (the Servicing Agency), through the Technology Transformation Services (TTS) within the Federal Acquisitions Service (FAS), builds effective, user-centric digital services focused on the interaction between Government and the people and businesses it serves. TTS helps agencies deliver on their mission through the development of digital and web services.

Cloud.gov is a platform-as-a-service developed by TTS to help federal agencies deliver digital services and operate efficiently at-scale in a cloud-hosted environment. Cloud.gov automates the security settings for any web service and configures the service to meet federal security requirements. Cloud.gov also provides agencies with a user interface that displays data such as usage metrics and how many servers are running to allow agencies to easily monitor and manage their usage of cloud services, which would otherwise require multiple layers of staffing. Cloud.gov provides agencies with templates for cloud security documentation to make the Authority to Operate (ATO) process more efficient for agencies.

TTS will provide access to the cloud.gov platform and its capabilities and maintains the cloud.gov platform and infrastructure security compliance up to the application level and agencies access the platform on a self-service level to host their systems.

The Requesting Agency, for purposes of this Statement of Work (SOW), is the AGENCY (AGENCY Acronym), (office). Agency, Office seeks access to cloud.gov in order to provide efficient hosting and achieve cost savings in deployment of software using cloud services.

2.0 SCOPE OF WORK AND DELIVERABLES

TTS will:

1. Provide Requesting Agency access to the cloud.gov platform and its capabilities.

2. Provision the Requesting Agency with the appropriate user accounts for cloud.gov and supporting cloud services

3. Provide the Requesting Agency with the login credentials for cloud.gov and supporting cloud services

4. Provide the Requesting Agency usage logs for cloud.gov and supporting cloud services

5. Provide the Requesting Agency with a basic level of support that includes on-site training, portfolio analysis, replatforming assistance, priority support, and co-development of custom services (Note: additional support is available on a reimbursable basis)

6. TTS will supply the Requesting Agency with monthly billing statements and will provide status updates upon request by the Requesting Agency.
